    Rose Byrne Explains Why She &amp; Bobby Cannavale Haven't Gotten Married Yet

    Rose Byrne and Bobby Cannavale are in no rush to get married. After nine years together and two kids, the 41-year-old Bridesmaids actress is explaining why she and the 51-year-old Will &amp; Grace actor havent gotten married yet.

    26 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Rose Byrne and Bobby Cannavale are in no rush to get married.

    After nine years together and two kids, the 41-year-old Bridesmaids actress is explaining why she and the 51-year-old Will & Grace actor haven’t gotten married yet.

    Click inside to read more…

    “I keep going, ‘Let’s get around to it, let’s do it.’ And then, you know, you have a baby, and then, oh, there’s another baby,” Rose shared with Sydney Morning Herald.

    Rose and Bobby have been together since 2012, and share two sons, Rocco, 5, and Rafa, 3.

    “It was kind of like that for us,” Rose continued. “I love weddings, and I know people [for whom] it’s an important thing, and I respect that totally. I guess for us it’s just been, we didn’t do it, we’ll do it, then – no! Pandemic.”
