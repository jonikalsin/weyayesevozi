    Juanita Healy Obituary, Death Cause – It is with deep sorrow and heavy hearts that we must share the heartbreaking news of the passing of one of our cherished vendors here at the market, Juanita Healy. Juanita held a special place in our market community, not only as a vendor but also as a former board member, and her absence will be profoundly felt. Juanita’s presence brought warmth, camaraderie, and a sense of unity to our market. Her baked goods, made with love and care, delighted the taste buds of all who were fortunate enough to enjoy them. She was not just a baker; she was an artist who crafted her creations with passion and dedication.

    Her contributions to our market extended beyond her delicious offerings. As a former board member, Juanita played an integral role in shaping the direction and spirit of our community. Her wisdom, insights, and unwavering commitment to our shared vision will be remembered and cherished. The loss of Juanita leaves a void that cannot be easily filled. Her smile, her warm greetings, and her friendly conversations will be dearly missed by all who had the privilege of knowing her.

    During this difficult time, our hearts go out to Juanita’s family, friends, and loved ones. We share in your grief and offer our deepest condolences. Please know that you are not alone in your sorrow, and we are here to support you in any way we can. Juanita’s memory will forever be a part of our market, a reminder of the love and dedication she poured into our community. As we mourn her passing, let us also celebrate the beautiful legacy she leaves behind, one of warmth, friendship, and the joy of sharing good food and good company. Juanita Healy will always hold a special place in our hearts, and she will be truly missed.
