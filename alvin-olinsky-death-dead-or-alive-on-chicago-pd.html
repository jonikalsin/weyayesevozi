    Alvin Olinsky Death

    In Chicago P.D. Season 5 of the episode Homecoming, Olinsky died in the hospital from the stabbing. Alvin Olinsky, portrayed beautifully by Elias Koteas, died after significant blood loss. 

    Voight investigates Olinsky’s death and seeks revenge.

    The character ends up killing Olinsky’s murderer in a revenge quest. 

    Then-showrunner Rick Eid gave an interview with TV Line, saying:

    It was a decision evolving organically from the ongoing storyline between Voight and Woods. From a creative perspective, the writers and I kept sticking to the idea that Voight’s decision to kill Bingham should mean him something.

    As Voight admired and loved Olinsky so much, we knew over time to land on the notion that Olinsky should be the one to pay for Voight’s sin.

    The former showrunner said that the decision was extremely hard for him and the others as they think of Elias as a terrific Actor and a good person.

    From a business perspective, the idea may have worked for a certain time but lost a loyal fan base who kept the character near and dear to their hearts. 

    Also, Alvin Olinsky has been an integral part of the show since the beginning.

    However, Elias, the Actor who played Olinsky, handled the story like a pro and remained committed to the storyline giving his all to the emotional death scene. Fans still miss the character dearly.

    What Happened To Alvin Olinsky On Chicago PD?

    Alvin Olinsky, played by Canadian Actor Elias Koteas was a beloved member of the Intelligence Unit from Chicago P.D. Seasons 1 to 5.

    The close character to Voight shared a likelihood to go rogue with their Police work. Voight and Olinsky understood each other very well. 

    But in the end, their close relationship led to Olinsky’s tragic ending.

    In the Season 5 ending, Olinsky’s DNA was recovered on the body of  Bingham, played by Joseph Sikora. Bingham also killed Voight’s son, Justin.

    Even though Voight had killed Bingham seeking revenge, Olinsky was arrested in the process for the murder charge.

    Olinsky knowing the truth remained silent and stayed loyal to his friend.

    Sadly, a fellow inmate ended up stabbing him in prison, and he tragically died during surgery in the Homecoming episode of the Season 5 finale.
