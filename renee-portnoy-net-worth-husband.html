    Introduction

    Renee Portnoy (née Satherwaite) is an American equestrian professional and social media influencer. She is best known as the ex-wife of Barstool Sports founder, Dave Portnoy.

    The Origin Story

    Portnoy was born in Abington, Massachusetts, on May 5th, 1983.

    Her father, Dennis Satterthwaite, was an officer in the US Armed Forces, while her mother, Sheryl Satterthwaite, was a nurse.

    Her parents divorced when she was six years old.

    Renee also has a sister, Lauren Satterthwaite.

    Portnoy loved animals from a young age, and was particularly enamored of horses. This would later lead her to her chosen profession.

    Portnoy went to high school in her hometown of Abington; she later attended Boston University, where she obtained a BS in international relations and business.

    Professional Career


    Renee joined the animal health services company SmartPak in 2012, where she worked as a senior buyer.

    Portnoy was easily able to translate her love of horses and her previous experience into skills applicable to her new job, and she continues to work for the company to this day.

    Renee joined SmartPak as a senior buyer, and was responsible for overseeing contracts and making purchase orders for the company.

    After two years with the company, she became the Director of Merchandising – a title she still holds today.

    Renee’s Rescues


    Portnoy also runs a dog rescue shelter, Renee’s Rescues, in Nantucket, Boston. The shelter provides a safe place for unwanted puppies, and helps to rehome the puppies with caring new owners.

    In addition, Renee is involved with the Nantucket Island Safe Harbor for Animals, which acts as a foster home for neglected or unwanted animals.

    Portnoy frequently visits the shelter personally in order to take care of its wards.

    Portnoy shares much of her work with the animals on social media, and as a result, has gained a considerable following. On Instagram, she has 37,500 followers, while her Twitter account is followed by more than 32,000 people.

    Husband

    Renee Portnoy met Dave Portnoy in 2005 and quickly began dating. Dave Portnoy is the founder of the hugely popular sports blog Barstool Sports. Renee Portnoy and Dave Portnoy got engaged in 2008 and were married in 2009.
